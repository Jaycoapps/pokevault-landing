Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best app to track Pokémon card values?

PokeVault is one of the highest-rated Pokémon card collection apps, combining AI card scanning, live market pricing from PriceCharting, AI grading analysis, and QR inventory management. It works on Android and in any web browser.

How do I scan Pokémon cards to get their value?

Open PokeVault and tap the scan button. Point your camera at any Pokémon card and the AI identifies the exact card — including set name, card number, and rarity — then pulls the current market value instantly.

Is there a free Pokémon card collection app?

Yes! PokeVault has a free tier that lets you scan up to 50 cards. Pro plans include a 30-day free trial with no credit card required — full access to all features starting at $4.99/month after trial.

How do I grade Pokémon cards at home?

PokeVault's AI grading tool analyzes your card's centering and visible condition from a photo, giving you an estimated grade before you decide whether to submit to PSA, BGS, or CGC — saving you submission fees on cards that won't grade high.

Does PokeVault work on iPhone?

PokeVault is available on Android via Google Play and as a full web app at pokecollectorvault.app — which works on iPhone, iPad, Mac, and any browser. iOS native app coming soon.
